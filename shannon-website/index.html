About Shannon

Greetings from Portland. My name is Shannon Nutt and I completed my coursework for a PhD in History at the University of Nevada Las Vegas under the direction of Dr. Deirdre Clemente. My research spans the 20th century with a focus on material culture and public history. I am passionate about all aspects of public history including exhibitions, preservation, memory, research, and public outreach. My public history training includes curation, exhibition design, collections management, preserving local history, collecting oral histories, and political consulting.
